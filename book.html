The alchemist





The story of this book is wonderful. Completely attaching. It motivates us to persue our dream, no matter what the circumstances are. It tells us to stop living a boring life like that of sheeps. The shephard keeps on having a dream about a treasure hidden near to pyramids. When this begins to worry him much, he finally decides to look through it. Situations turn out like this that he sets off on his persue of treasure, leaving everything behind. And understanding the meaning of life.....

Five point someone





Its a story of 3 engineering students of IIT,who were roommates. The novel starts with a friend of them hospitalised, and rest two of them worried. When one among them, who is also the narrator decides that if their friend gets well, he will write a novel about their friendship. So, their friend gets well, and hence, the narrator shares their tale of friendship, their funny moments,their constant five point grades, their way of studying with the world.

The power of now





It is one of the best books you need to read if you are pne among those people, who are continuously struggling with their habit of constantly living in the past or future. Or if you are the one who thinks of imaginary situations which are unlikely to happen. The author here explains every question of your that your mind forces you to ask, while you are doing this
